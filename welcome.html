Welcome to Big Boss Characters 2025.

1.Shin-chan

Shin-chan, whose full name is Shinnosuke Nohara, is a funny and mischievous 5-year-old boy from Japan.
He loves dancing, eating snacks, and teasing his mom, Misae.
He lives in Kasukabe with his parents, baby sister Himawari, and his dog Shiro.
Shin-chan is known for his silly jokes, weird songs, and unpredictable behavior.
 Even though he causes trouble, everyone secretly loves him!

2.Himawari

Himawari, the baby sister of Shin-chan, loved her bright yellow rattle more than anything.
One day, she crawled around the living room and accidentally dropped her rattle under the sofa.
She tried to reach it but couldnt quite get it.
Just then, Shin-chan came to help.
With a big smile, he said, “Dont worry, Himawari! Im the big brother superhero!”
He carefully pulled out the rattle and gave it back to her.
 Himawari giggled happily and shook her rattle with joy.
Together, they played happily all afternoon.

3.Hiroshi

Hiroshi is a hardworking salaryman who works long hours to support his family.
He s often tired when he comes home, but still tries to be a good father.
Though he pretends to be strict, he's often outsmarted or teased by Shin-chan.
 He's known for his stinky socks, bad luck, and funny reactions.

4.Misae Nohara

Misae Nohara is Shin-chan's caring but short-tempered mother.
She is a housewife who tries to manage her mischievous son, baby daughter Himawari, and the household.
Misae is strict with Shin-chan but also deeply loves her family.
 Shes known for her quick temper, budget-conscious shopping, and funny scoldings.

5.Shiro

Shiro is a small, white, fluffy dog known for being smart, loyal, and calm — the exact opposite of Shin-chan!
He was originally found by Shin-chan in a cardboard box and brought home as a stray.
